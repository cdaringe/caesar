# Summary

This is a photogrammetry 3D scan that I edited for 3D printing. It is part of a larger statue of Julius Caesar at the Louvre by Benjamin Bardou but I cut it off (it's not a complete scan) so it's a bust. I smoothed and sculpted some areas and filled holes. I also scaled and oriented the model.

The original scan can be found here: https://sketchfab.com/models/30f43907f7814582b5dacbbadbabbe6d#
